We have the afternoon for lunch, a walking tour of the old city center - a UNESCO site - and to explore some on your own. Our overnight location is in the mountains south of Salzburg. Day 2: The picturesque Salzkammergut lake district

The lake district east of Salzburg is a combination of wonderful scenery and picturesque villages.
