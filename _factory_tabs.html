Day 1: Arrival in Munich

We'll pick you up at your arrival flight in Munich and bring you along the northern rim of the Alps to lovely Salzburg. We have the afternoon for lunch, a walking tour of the old city center - a UNESCO site - and to explore some on your own. Our overnight location is in the mountains south of Salzburg. Day 2: The picturesque Salzkammergut lake district

The lake district east of Salzburg is a combination of wonderful scenery and picturesque villages. We'll see the Red Bull HQ at Lake Fuschl, have a boat ride across another lake to the quaint village of St. Wolfgang and visit iconic Hallstatt, where we take the funicular up for a panoramic view of the lake and optional salt mine tour.
